In a sport game between two teams, there are only 3 possible outcomes and out of that 3 only one will happen in a single match. All outcomes are mutually exclusive from each other. For example,Team A will only win,draw or lose against Team B, so there is 33,33% chance among each possible outcome.

PLACING BETS

When a gambler assume that team A will win a match against team B, he/she will have 33,33% chances of succeeding which is unlikely to happen but if the gambler assume that team A wins Or draw against team B now the gambler will have 66,66% chances of succeeding which is likely to happen.

This prediction model aims to provide gamblers with 66,66% chances of winning.
